Goal Setting 101

Why S.M.A.R.T.?

Most goals fail because they are vague ("I want to be rich"). Your brain can't act on vague instructions.

  • Specific (Who, what, where?)
  • Measurable (How much? How many?)
  • Achievable (Is it realistic?)
  • Relevant (Does it matter to you?)
  • Time-Bound (When is the deadline?)
