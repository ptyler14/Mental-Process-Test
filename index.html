The Mental Bank Ledger

What is it?

A nightly journaling practice where you "pay" yourself for positive habits.

Why use it?

To break through financial or success "ceilings" by reprogramming your inner script.

How to do it?

Every night before bed, log your daily "Value Events" and calculate your fantasy deposit.

The Placemat Process

What is it?

A task management tool that splits your to-do list into two columns: things YOU will do, and things the UNIVERSE will handle.

Why use it?

To relieve overwhelm and anxiety by consciously delegating tasks.

How to do it?

List tasks you MUST do today in the "Me" column. Put everything else in the "Universe" column.

The Segment Intending Process

What is it?

A practice of "pre-paving" your vibrational path by setting a clear intention before entering a new segment of time.

Why use it?

To reduce resistance and confusion, creating smoother, more enjoyable experiences.

How to do it?

Identify when you are changing activities. Pause. Ask yourself: "What do I want from this period?" State it clearly, then proceed.

The Focus Wheel Process

What is it?

A visual tool to bridge the gap between a negative thought and a positive desire.

Why use it?

When you feel resistance or negative emotion, this helps you "slow down the wheel" of negativity and spin up positive momentum.

How to do it?

Identify your desire in the center. Then, find 12 general, true statements that feel good to you. Place them around the wheel until you feel relief.

The Simpson Protocol

What is it?

An advanced hypnosis method that communicates directly with your Superconscious Mind to clear deep-seated issues without you needing to speak.

Why use it?

To access profound states of healing (Esdaile state) and clear physical, emotional, or spiritual blocks at their root cause.

How to do it?

Lie down with your hand near the Spacebar. Listen to the guided audio. When asked a Yes/No question by your Superconscious, tap Spacebar for YES.
