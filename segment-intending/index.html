Segment Intending

Pre-paving your vibrational path.

What is a Segment?

A new segment begins anytime your intentions change, even slightly. Examples include:

  • When the phone rings.
  • When you get into your car.
  • When someone walks into the room.
  • Transitioning from work to rest.

Why Intend?

By pausing to set an intention before you enter a new segment, you "pre-pave" your vibrational path. This reduces resistance, confusion, and overwhelm, allowing you to move through your day with clarity and flow.
