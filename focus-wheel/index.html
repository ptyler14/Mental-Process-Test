How to Use the Focus Wheel

  1. Identify your desire: What do you want to feel? Write this in the center of the wheel.
  2. Create 12 belief statements: Write positive statements you already believe that match your desire. Start at the 12 o'clock position.
  3. Focus on each statement: Use the 17-second timer to focus on each statement. This builds vibrational momentum.
  4. Assess your relief: As you move around the wheel, notice when you feel a sense of relief.
  5. Complete the circuit: After all 12 statements, you should feel closer to your original desire.

Example Statements

If your desire is to feel slender:

  • "For the most part, my body's doing all right."
  • "I know that my physical body responds to my thoughts."
  • "I like taking control of my own experience."

Key Principles

Statements must be:

  • Believable - You must already believe them
  • General - Not too specific to avoid resistance
  • Matching - Aligned with your central desire
